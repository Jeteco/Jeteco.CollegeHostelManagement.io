Hostel Requirements

    These are the facilities which should be provided by hostel management system
  • Well ventilated rooms.

  • Well-lit and well spaced.

  • Mosquito nets provided.

  • A wardrobe in a 2 seater room & two wardrobe in a 4 seater room.

  • Conductive study area: equivalent to the number of residents in Hostel.

  • There should be proper electricity supply in each and every room of hostel.

  • The rooms should be cleaned and There should be proper curriculum of cleaning schedule of Hostel.

  • The rooms of hostel should be well furnished with at least a table-chair should be available for every student.

  • Mess food should be healthy and be made with complete cleanliness.

    • Note :- Only those problems which are related to these facilities can be resolved by the system
